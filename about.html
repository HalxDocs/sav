Our Story

From humble beginnings to culinary excellence

The Savory Bites Journey

Founded in 2010 by Chef Maria Rodriguez, Savory Bites began as a small bistro with just 10 tables and a simple philosophy: create extraordinary dining experiences using the freshest local ingredients.

What started as a neighborhood favorite has grown into one of the city's most celebrated restaurants, earning recognition from Food & Wine magazine and three consecutive Michelin Bib Gourmand awards.

Today, we continue to honor our roots while innovating with seasonal menus that showcase both traditional techniques and contemporary flavors.

Our Milestones

1

2010 - Humble Beginnings

Chef Maria opens the original Savory Bites location in a converted warehouse space with just 10 tables and a staff of four.

2

2013 - First Recognition

Featured in "Best New Restaurants" by City Eats magazine and receives our first Michelin Bib Gourmand award.

3

2015 - Expansion

Moved to our current location with expanded dining room and open kitchen concept. Launched our farm-to-table program.

4

2018 - Sustainability Award

Recognized by the Green Restaurant Association for our sustainable practices and zero-waste initiatives.

5

2023 - Present Day

Celebrating over a decade of service with our expanded team of 35, while maintaining the intimate dining experience that started it all.

Our Philosophy

Seasonal Ingredients

We source 90% of our ingredients from local farms and purveyors, changing our menu seasonally to showcase the best each season offers.

Sustainable Practices

From composting to energy-efficient equipment, we're committed to minimizing our environmental impact at every step.

Community Focus

We believe restaurants should be gathering places that nourish both body and community through food, education, and shared experiences.
